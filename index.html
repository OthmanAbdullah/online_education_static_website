Introduction 



Online education is a flexible instructional 
delivery system that encompasses any kind 
of learning that takes place via the Internet. 
Online learning gives educators an opportunity
to reach students who may not be able to 
enroll in a traditional classroom course 
and supports students who need to work on their
own schedule and at their own pace. 
Online learning is becoming a more traditional 
approach to achieving educational goals for
a number of reasons.
